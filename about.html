About Me

Hi, I'm Topher! I'm a librarian, information designer, and dataviz wonk, working with technology to make information and data accessible to everyone.
I currently work as the Instructional Technology and Assessment Librarian at Georgetown University. If you want lots of detail on how I got here, view my Curriculum Vitae.

As well as hosting my portfolio, this site is a place for me to figure out how to build stuff on the web (See Porfolio: Projects for things I've been playing with lately).

When I'm not coding, I'm a musician, runner, gamer, and occasional foodie. Keep an eye out for fun stuff!

About the website

This site was initially created 24 July 2016.

The full codebase is open source, and on Github--feel free to view the source code or revision history, and borrow what you like!

I write all of my code in Atom, and use SCSS to mark up my stylesheets.

I use Cinzel and Rambla for my headers, Volkhov and Inconsolata for my other text, all thanks to fonts.google.com

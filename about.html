About Me

Hi, I'm Topher! I'm a librarian, information designer, and dataviz wonk, working with technology to make information and data accessible to everyone.


I currently work as the Instructional Technology and Assessment Librarian at Georgetown University. For far-too-much detail on my background, view my Curriculum Vitae.


When I'm not teaching or coding, I'm a musician, runner, and gamer. Various projects relating to these will pop up on occasion. :-)

About the website

This site was initially created 24 July 2016, to host my portfolio and digital projects I want to showcase.

The full codebase is open source, and on Github--feel free to view the source code or revision history, and borrow what you like!

I write all of my code in Atom, and use SCSS to mark up my stylesheets.

I use Cinzel and Rambla for my headers, Volkhov and Inconsolata for my other text, all thanks to Google Fonts
